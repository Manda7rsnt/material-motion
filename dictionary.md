# Motion Dictionary

This is a definition of the Terms used by the Material Motion team in their Expressions.

    Term: fadeIn()
    Modifiers:
    - .from(float)
    - .to(float)

Fade an element's opacity in.

    Term: fadeIn()
    Modifiers:
    - .from(float)
    - .to(float)

Fade an element's opacity out.
